Anime is a distinct style of Japanese animation characterized by 2D, hand-drawn, or computer-assisted art, featuring, colorful graphics, vibrant characters, and detailed, often emotional, storytelling. While in Japan, "anime" refers to all animation, outside Japan, it specifically means animation produced in Japan.
